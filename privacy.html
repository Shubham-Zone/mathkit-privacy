Privacy Policy

At MathKit, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines what data we do or do not collect, how it is used, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Information Collection

  • No Personal Data Collected: MathKit does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as name, email, address, or device ID.
  • No Usage Analytics: We do not track your app usage, behavior, or preferences.

2. Data Security & Offline Usage

  • Offline-Only: The app works entirely offline and does not require an internet connection.
  • No Server Communication: All math operations and user interactions are handled on your local device.

3. Permissions

  • Minimal Permissions: If storage or camera access is requested in future updates, it will only be to enable key features like saving solutions or scanning math problems from paper.
  • No Background Tracking: The app does not access your location, contacts, or any background data.

4. Children’s Privacy

  • Child-Friendly: MathKit is safe for users of all ages, including children.
  • COPPA Compliance: We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and do not knowingly collect any data from users under 13.

5. Advertising & Monetization

  • Ad-Free Experience: MathKit is free of ads and third-party SDKs.
  • No In-App Purchases: We don’t offer in-app purchases that collect billing information.

6. Data Sharing & Third Parties

  • No Third-Party Access: We do not share any data with external parties, advertisers, analytics providers, or affiliates.

7. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in the app’s functionality. You will be notified of any significant changes via the app or this website.
